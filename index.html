💡 What do the settings mean?
Signal strength (Buy score) — How confident the AI needs to be before buying. Higher = fewer but stronger signals. Default 65.
Max coins to hold — How many different coins to own at once. More = more spread. Default 6.
Trade size — What % of your balance goes into each coin. Default 40% (aggressive).
Stop loss — If a coin drops this much from your buy price, the bot sells automatically to protect you. Default -10%.
Trail stop — Once a coin is profitable, if it drops X% from its highest price the bot sells. Locks in profit. Default 12%.
